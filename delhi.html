New Delhi

Time to explore the capital city of India

Indian Accent, Lodhi Road

Indian Accent is a fine dining restaurant located in Lodhi Road, New Delhi. It is known for its innovative Indian cuisine and has received numerous awards for its culinary excellence.

Qutub Minar, Mehrauli

The Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the tallest brick minarets in the world. It is located in Mehrauli, Delhi, and is a popular tourist attraction.

India Gate, Kartavya Path

India Gate is a war memorial located in New Delhi. It was built in honor of the soldiers who died in World War I and is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.
